RBI Monetary Policy Meeting LIVE: The Reserve Bank of India’s (RBI) announced its fifth bi-monthly monetary policy of FY25, today, December 6. The six-member Monetary Policy Committee (MPC) led by RBI Governor Shaktikanta Das decided to keep the benchmark repo rate unchanged at 6.5% for the eleventh straight meeting, and maintain the monetary policy stance ‘Neutral’. Moreover, the MPC slashed the cash reserve ratio (CRR) by 50 basis points (bps) to 4%. Stay tuned to our RBI Monetary Policy Meeting Live blog for the latest updates.
